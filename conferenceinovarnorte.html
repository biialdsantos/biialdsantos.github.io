conference inovar+norte

As part of the International Conference “Sustainability: The Future of SMEs”, held on June 27 in Vila Real, I was responsible for the entire graphic design of the event. This project included the creation of:

  • Customized notebooks
  • Themed tote bags
  • Identification badges
  • Signage for the venue
  • Posts and visual content for social media

The event was promoted under the INOVAR+ 2.0 project, developed by Nervir – Vila Real Business Association and Nerba, with the support of Acisat – Business Association, and co-financed by NORTE 2020, Portugal 2020 and the European Union through the ERDF.

The visual identity was designed to reflect the values of sustainability, innovation, and SME growth, ensuring coherence between physical and digital materials and providing participants with a clear and appealing communication experience.
