otzi app

Otzi is a mobile application dedicated to the tattoo world, designed to simplify the connection between tattoo artists and enthusiasts. The name is inspired by Ötzi, the mummy of a man from the Copper Age discovered in the Alps in 1991, who lived over 5,000 years ago and had around 61 tattoos. The name symbolizes the ancestral and historical connection between humanity and tattooing, bringing authenticity and cultural depth to the brand identity.

main task

The main task was to design a functional and intuitive mobile application that meets the needs of both tattoo artists and clients. The goal was to provide efficient appointment management, direct communication, and professional exposure, while helping users discover tattoo artists by location and style preferences.
