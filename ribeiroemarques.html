ribeiro & marques

Ribeiro & Marques, Lda. began its activity in 1988 with its first store in Caldas da Rainha. Today, we have a strong presence in the Western Region of Portugal, with six additional branches.

We are authorized distributors of leading and renowned brands such as Dyrup, Cin, and Robbialac — our trusted partners in delivering high-quality products for the construction and industrial painting sectors.
