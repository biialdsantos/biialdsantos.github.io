about_me

I’m Beatriz Santos, a 23-year-old graphic and multimedia designer from Caldas da Rainha, Portugal. With a background in Audiovisual and Multimedia, I’m currently completing a Bachelor’s degree in Graphic and Multimedia Design at ESAD.CR, where I’ve been developing strong skills across various areas of design.

Since 2020, I’ve been working at Training Partners, creating visual content – from social media design, video editing, and motion graphics to website development in WordPress and UX/UI design. I also completed an internship at Plural (TVI), where I focused on video editing.

I’m proficient in tools such as Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects, Premiere Pro, InDesign, and Figma, and I enjoy combining creativity with functionality in every project. I’m always open to new challenges that allow me to grow and create visual impact.
